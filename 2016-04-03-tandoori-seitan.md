For seitan:

  * ¼ cup nutritional yeast
  * ¼ cup hulled moong dal
  * ¼ cup garbanzo flour
  * ¾ cup vital wheat gluten
  * 1 cup water

&nbsp;

For simmering liquid:

  * 4 cups water
  * 1/3 cup light shoyu
  * 1 ½ cups dry white wine
  * 1” ginger, slivered
  * 1 stick cinnamon
  * ½ tbsp tamarind concentrate
