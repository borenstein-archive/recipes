2 tbsp rhubarb liqueur

2 tbsp vodka or white rum

2 tbsp lemon juice

2 tbsp seltzer

&nbsp;

Combine all but seltzer in a cocktail shaker half filled with ice. Shake vigorously and pour over the seltzer.
