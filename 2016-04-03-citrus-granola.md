_I let these cook for several hours at 250F by accident. Something funny happened: 100% of the citrus taste left the granola, and I was left with a pleasant-but-bland graham cracker flavor. Weird.__ _

  * 1 ½  cup oat bran
  * 1 ½  cup rolled oats
  * ¼ cup sugar
  * 1/3 cup olive oil
  * 1 large egg
  * 1/3 cup egg white
  * ¼ tsp salt
