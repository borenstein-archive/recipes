14 oz brown mustard seeds

2 ½ cup Chardonnay

2 ½ cup red vinegar

3 tbsp and a bit of honey

A little less than 1tbsp salt

Put all this in a ½ gallon jar and wait a few days. Grind it to desired consistency and wait another week before eating.
