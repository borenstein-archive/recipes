Pure as the driven snow; thick, zesty and delicious. And it takes about 15 seconds to prepare.

<span style="font-weight: bold;">INGREDIENTS:</span> 

  * 2 shallots, peeled
  * 1/2 cup balsamic vinegar
  * 3 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  * 1 tbsp dijon mustard
  * 1/2 tsp dried tarragon
  * 1/4 tsp salt

<span style="font-weight: bold;">DIRECTIONS:</p> 

<p>
  <span style="font-weight: bold;"></span></span>Combine all ingredients in a blender until smooth, about 10 seconds. Serve on any green salad.<span style="font-weight: bold;"><br /></span>
</p>
