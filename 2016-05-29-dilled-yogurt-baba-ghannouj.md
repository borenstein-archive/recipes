This is a derivative recipe&#8211;it assumes you already have baba ghannouj. (I know I need to write that up.)

Ingredients:

  * 1 cup baba ghannouj
  * 2/3 cup Fage 2% yogurt
  * Handful of dill

Finely mince dill. Combine all ingredients thoroughly. Serve chilled.
